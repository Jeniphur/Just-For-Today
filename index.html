Just for Today


Here to celebrate your daily wins every step of the way!


Our purpose is to help you take the steps you need to make your personal self-care a part of your daily life.

Many of us tend to put ourselves last and focus on others, but you can’t forget to give yourself the love and care you give others! With this in mind “Just for Today” gives you a li’l buddy to support your self-care journey. Every small step you make is celebrated, and our app gives you activities you can do to be healthy in all areas of your life, physical, mental, emotional, social, and spirtual.

As you cross-off your goals you can spoil your new friend with accessories and gifts. Caring for them as you care for yourself and soon enough self-care will be present in your life without even realizing it!

So jump right in and download our app to meet the li’l buddy who is going to help you take care of yourself each day and every step of the way!
